Global & Tax Financial Services

Taxation, being one of the most intricate aspects of conducting business globally, requires expert handling. At Swift O Pay, we go the extra mile to manage your tax obligations, including sales tax, value-added tax (VAT), VAT management, and remittance. We are steadfast in our commitment to alleviate the complexities that the intricate nature of tax imposes on your business operations. Our goal is to streamline your financial processes, allowing you to focus on your core business objectives.

As previously stated, taxation, especially sales tax, is a complex aspect of business operations. At Swift O Pay, we are taking proactive measures to ensure that you can fully leverage the benefits of tax laws and their implementation. Our aim is to provide you with a fully compliant international buying and selling experience. To achieve this, we are diligently examining the following aspects

  • Track European VAT rates, for countries directly in European countries or outside European countries.
  • Manage your sales tax in accordance with US laws.
  • Assist you to comply with regulations in the Asia Pacific and the Middle East region.
  • Automatic calculation of all your taxes and remit your taxes whenever needed.
  • We are looking at the structure of various countries and noting all changes from time to time.



Refunds and Chargebacks

Swift O Pay provides an extensive suite of refund management services that your team can leverage in the event of a refund. Our team of specialists is dedicated to helping you maintain control over chargebacks and uphold your esteemed reputation with credit card companies. Our team members are equipped to vigorously contest each claim, providing you with strategies and tactics to safeguard your business against future claims. Upon successful resolution of such claims, our team ensures the proper disbursement of repayments and updates the sales record accordingly. Our commitment is to provide a professional, efficient, and reliable service that caters to your business needs.
